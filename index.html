In the years that followed, during the rule of the Galactic Empire, I became a smuggler and the co-pilot of Captain Han Solo aboard the Millennium Falcon. We worked in the employ of crime lord Jabba Desilijic Tiure until Solo was forced to dump a shipment of spice to avoid trouble with the Empire, at which point a bounty was placed on our heads. Soon thereafter, I and Solo were hired by Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi to ferry him, Luke Skywalker, C-3PO, and R2-D2 to Alderaan on a mission vital to the survival of the Alliance to Restore the Republic.
